主題選書

  • 提供多元主題選書，若有建議或推薦，歡迎讓我們知道！
    Bring in books in fiction & literature, and nonfiction. Any suggestions or recommendations are welcome.
  • 每期100冊，每三個月更新一批選書。
    A New selection of 100 books every 3 months.
  • 首週【館內展示】，第二週起可【外借14天】，無人預約可續借。
    On display in the library for the first week, available for loan from the second week. The loan period is 14 days, and renewals are permitted unless the item is reserved for another patron.
